3. Third-Party Services

Important: To provide our Service, your data is processed by the following third-party services. Each has their own privacy policy.

3.1 OpenAI (Whisper API)

We use OpenAI's Whisper API for speech-to-text transcription. Audio from your videos is sent to OpenAI for processing.

  • Privacy Policy: openai.com/privacy
  • Data is processed according to OpenAI's API data usage policies

3.2 Replicate (Chatterbox TTS)

We use Replicate's hosted Chatterbox model for text-to-speech synthesis with voice cloning.

  • Privacy Policy: replicate.com/privacy
  • Voice samples and text are sent for audio generation

3.3 Google Translate

We use Google Translate (via deep-translator) for text translation.

  • Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy
  • Transcript text is sent for translation
